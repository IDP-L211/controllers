coordtransform_world_to_map(vec: list) → list¶

Transfer the world coordinate (East, North) to image coordinate

The image coordinate has (0,0) at the top left corner and (width-1,height-1) at the bottom right corner. Therefore, the North coordinate needs to be reversed. The origin also need to be shifted to the center.
